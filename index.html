7th Conference on Sustainability in Civil Engineering (CSCE’25 An International Conference)

Conference Overview

Welcome to the Conference on Sustainability in Civil Engineering (CSCE), organized by the Department of Civil Engineering at Capital University of Science and Technology, Islamabad, Pakistan. The conference focuses on highlighting sustainability in the field of civil engineering. It aims to provide a platform for civil engineers from academia and industry to share practical experiences and research findings in their respective specializations. We hope all participants find this an excellent opportunity for academic and industrial communities to address emerging challenges, exchange solutions, and discuss future research directions. The conference features multiple parallel sessions covering various specialties, fostering interaction among researchers and engineers to enhance their understanding of sustainability in civil engineering.

Conference Venue

Capital University of Science & Technology (CUST) Islamabad Pakistan was established in 1998 as Islamabad Campus of Mohammad Ali Jinnah University (MAJU) Karachi and achieved its recognition in the country and abroad by maintaining excellent academic standards. CUST is a comprehensive Institution which have faculties including Engineering, Medicines, Natural Sciences, Management, Computing, Humanities and Arts. Faculty of Engineering has three Departments: Electrical Engineering; Mechanical Engineering and Civil Engineering. The Department of Civil Engineering is dedicated to produce quality professional engineers with abilities to design, manage and operate Civil Engineering based fields. It is due to quality education that Capital University of Science and Technology has achieved the honour for being among top few Pakistani universities and the first among private institutions whose BS Civil Engineering Program is accredited on Outcome Based Assessment (OBA) by Pakistan Engineering Council. The department is also offering graduate programs in four specialization areas.

Submission Guidelines

The CSCE ‘25 conference invites submissions of original research, review papers, and case studies with a length of 6 to 8 pages. Papers must be submitted in both Word and PDF formats via email to csce@cust.edu.pk. All submissions will undergo a peer review process, and authors are advised to strictly follow the provided template to avoid any inconvenience during the review. The conference enforces a strict plagiarism policy, requiring an overall similarity of less than 20% and a single-source similarity of less than 5%. Authors must also submit signed Declaration Forms in the prescribed format along with their final submissions before registration. The paper template and copyright form can be accessed on the conference website (csce.cust.edu.pk). Guidelines for oral and poster presentations will be communicated through the paper acceptance email. All accepted papers will be published in the ISBN proceedings.
